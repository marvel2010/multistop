MultiStop

MultiStop is a tool for planning pickups and dropoffs at public transit stops!

MultiStop assumes that one person is driving (the "driver") and the other person is taking public transit (the "passenger").

In Pickup mode, the driver and passenger start in separate places, meet at a transit stop, and then go to a destination together.

In Dropoff mode, the driver and the passenger start together, the passenger gets dropped off at a transit stop, then the driver and passenger go to separate destinations.
